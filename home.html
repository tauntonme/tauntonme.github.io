Signals for West Buckland Miniature Railway

The Signalling System - Promoting safer running of our railways


Taunton Model Engineers are working on designing and building a signalling system.

How are we doing? Here's a progress report - Signalling - A system design for Safety, Flexibility, Simplicity. Published Jan 2024

Update March 2024 -

These pages are to contain every detail of the design and build as it progresses, 'Open Sourcing' offers the hope that others will be able to inspect and understand all aspects of the design and construction, and to get involved themselves.


The West Buckland site remains a mud-fest, meaning progress installing hard signalling infrastucture lags behind the detailed electronic and software system design. This is now complete, with controllers, LED signals and communication networks all built and working "on the bench". Yet to do - reach decisions on points actuation methods, and train detection modules.

TME Signalling Team members Please Note - You are encouraged to add to and modify the content saved here.

Ask J.F about joining the team of 'collaborators'. It's not too difficult, it goes like this :

  1. J.F. uses your email address to 'invite' you to join in GitHub collaboration
  2. GitHub email you an invite to sign up
  3. Should you be agreeable and sign up, you will gain read/write acces to the file repository!

For the basics of using 'GitHub', see loads of help and tutorials on Youtube.
